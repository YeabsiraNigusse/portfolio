Dr. Akililu Lemma

The man who saved millions of lives


Aklilu Lemma was a young Ethiopian doctor who discovered a natural treatment against bilharzia, also known as schistosomiasis, a debilitating and eventually fatal illness..

Here's a time line of Dr. Akililu's life:

  • Born - 18 september 1935 Jijiga Ethiopia
  • Died - 5 April 1997(aged 61)
  • Nationality - Ethiopian
  • Alma mater - Addis Ababa University College
  • Awards - Right livelihood Award
  • Fields - Phatobiologist
